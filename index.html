Big Data

Conventional or “normal” business applications rely on structured data with predetermined links between data sets from different systems. For example, customer details in your ecommerce system is linked, via a customer ID field, with customer details in you CRM. These and other systems produce data which is valuable when harvested intelligently and cost-effectively.
